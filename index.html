About me

I am a Master's student in Robotics at University of Pennsylvania. I am excited about solving challenging problems in controls and navigation for robots. My research at DAIR Lab is focused on developing safety-critical controllers for bipedal robots to balance by using Sum-Of-Squares programming.

I have a Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Trichy.
